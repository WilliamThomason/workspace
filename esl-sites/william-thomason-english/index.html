Teacher Language Levels Explained

Filter teachers by their certified level in your native language using the CEFR scale:

A1-A2 (Basic) — Can explain simple vocabulary and basic grammar concepts in your language.
B1 (Intermediate) — Can handle main points on familiar matters and explain grammar concepts clearly.
B2 (Upper Intermediate) — Can explain subtle differences, technical vocabulary, and complex grammar in your language.
C1-C2 (Advanced/Native) — Full fluency. Can explain any concept including idioms, nuance, and industry terminology.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?▼
Book a free 30-minute session with William. He will assess your level, discuss your goals, and show you how the platform works. No payment required.
What is the AI translation widget?▼
When a concept cannot be explained in English alone, AI translates key terms into your home language in real-time during the lesson. This is included in every session at no extra cost.
How is this different from large platforms like iTalki or Preply?▼
Large platforms offer generic courses, bright interfaces, and teachers who follow a one-size-fits-all curriculum. William Thomason English is built specifically for IT professionals and their families — dark-mode-first, tailored curricula, and a teacher who understands your world.
What does B1 or B2 mean for teacher language levels?▼
These are CEFR levels. B1 means the teacher can handle main points on familiar matters. B2 means they can explain subtle differences and technical vocabulary. Click the info icon next to language level in pricing for a full breakdown.
How do bundles work?▼
Buy 12, 24, 48, or 64 sessions upfront and save 10-25%. Sessions never expire. Use them at your own pace. The discount applies to the base rate.
Can I cancel or reschedule?▼
Cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your session for a full refund. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) receive 50%. No-shows are charged the full rate.
Do you teach children too?▼
Yes. William teaches the whole family. Lessons for children are designed to be engaging and age-appropriate, with progress tracking parents can follow.
What exams do you prepare for?▼
IELTS, TOEFL, Cambridge (B2 First, C1 Advanced), OET, PTE, Duolingo English Test, CELPIP, TOEIC, and more. All major English exams are covered.
